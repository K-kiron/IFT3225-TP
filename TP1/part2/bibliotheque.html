THE GREAT GATSBY Fitzgerald 2014 32 CAD I thought that the book was all right. The story was bland for my liking but I did like the social commentary that the book provided, dealing with the issues of their time, alcohol, wealth, and partying. While I do not call this a fun read, I do recommend it be read so that the reader can get a taste of life in the 1920s. The book can be confusing at times and needs to be read carefully or reread to fully understand.
